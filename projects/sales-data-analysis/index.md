### What I built
- Cleaned transactional sales data and standardized geographic identifiers for mapping
- Time-series views that show seasonal peaks, promotions, and growth trends
- Regional dashboards that compare product performance across markets

### Approach
1. **Data hygiene:** validated currencies, handled missing prices, and reconciled duplicate SKUs.  
2. **Exploration:** built month-over-month comparisons, moving averages, and cohort-style charts for new products.  
3. **Geo-visualization:** used choropleths and bar charts to highlight territories driving the most revenue.

### Results
- Clear picture of high-performing products by season and geography
- Recommendations for inventory focus in markets with accelerating demand
- Visuals that make it easy for business teams to act on the findings
