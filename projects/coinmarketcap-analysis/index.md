## Overview

This project provides a comprehensive analysis and visualization of cryptocurrency market data from CoinMarketCap. It explores price trends, market capitalizations, and correlations between major cryptocurrencies using advanced data science techniques.

The analysis focuses on understanding the volatile cryptocurrency market through data-driven insights, helping investors and enthusiasts make informed decisions based on historical patterns and statistical relationships.

## Methodology

### 1. Data Collection
- Gathered comprehensive cryptocurrency market data from CoinMarketCap
- Automated data pipeline for continuous updates
- Ensured data quality through validation checks

### 2. Data Preprocessing
- Cleaned and structured raw market data
- Handled missing values and outliers
- Normalized data for consistent analysis

### 3. Analysis & Visualization
- Performed statistical analysis on price trends
- Created time-series visualizations of market behavior
- Analyzed market cap distributions and growth patterns
- Investigated correlations between major cryptocurrencies
