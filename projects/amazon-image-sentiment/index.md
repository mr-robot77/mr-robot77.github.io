### What I built
- Convolutional neural network to categorize Amazon product images across multiple classes
- NLP pipeline that cleans reviews, extracts n-grams, and classifies sentiment
- Visual artifacts (word clouds, top-brand charts) that make review insights tangible

### Approach
1. **Computer vision:** fine-tuned CNN architectures, tracked accuracy across epochs, and mitigated class imbalance.  
2. **Text analytics:** tokenized and lemmatized reviews, engineered polarity features, and trained classic ML models.  
3. **Reporting:** paired image predictions with sentiment summaries to highlight quality gaps by brand/category.

### Results
- Multi-modal view of product quality that combines visual and textual signals
- Reusable training scripts for both CV and NLP tasks
- Insightful visuals that help business stakeholders prioritize product improvements
