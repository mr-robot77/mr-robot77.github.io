### What I built
- Cleaned daily cryptocurrency listings from CoinMarketCap to create an analysis-ready dataset
- Visualized market-cap dominance and price trends to reveal macro movements in the market
- Explored correlations between leading assets to understand how coins move together

### Approach
1. **Data preparation:** removed duplicates, normalized column names, and converted timestamps for time-series analysis.  
2. **Exploratory analysis:** profiled volatility, rolling averages, and dispersion in market caps to spot momentum shifts.  
3. **Insight storytelling:** built comparison plots for BTC vs. altcoins, and heatmaps that highlight relationships between assets.

### Results
- Clear visual narrative of market behavior over the study period
- Correlation structure that highlights when diversification benefits shrink
- Reusable notebook and charts for presenting insights to non-technical stakeholders
