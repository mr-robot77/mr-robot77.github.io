## Overview

This project focuses on collecting, processing, and visualizing cryptocurrency market data sourced from **CoinMarketCap**. The goal was to extract meaningful insights from large volumes of market data and communicate them through clear, informative visualizations.

## Methodology

- **Data Collection**: Fetched historical market data for the top cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and others.
- **Data Cleaning**: Handled missing values, standardized formats, and ensured data consistency using Pandas.
- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**: Investigated distributions, trends, and relationships in price and market cap data.
- **Visualization**: Created a range of static and interactive charts using Matplotlib and Seaborn to highlight key patterns.

## Key Findings

- Strong positive correlation observed between Bitcoin and Ethereum price movements.
- Market cap distributions are heavily right-skewed, with a small number of coins dominating total market value.
- Price volatility peaks coincide with major market events and news cycles.
